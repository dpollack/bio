Dan is recognized throughout the storage industry for his reputation
as a thought leader in storage and high performance, reliable, cost
efficient infrastructures. Dan has over 20 years’ experience managing,
architecting and maintaining enterprise class web scale infrastructures
utilizing cutting edge technologies. He is a multidisciplinary technology
expert with a focus on storage systems at scale, a proven ability
to identify, troubleshoot, and mitigate operational issues ensuring
customer satisfaction and high availability. In 2001 he leveraged
his vast experience and knowledge to author "Practical Storage Area
Networking." When Dan isn’t busy delving into new technologies,
setting organizational direction or advising clients, he enjoys playing
Ice Hockey, hiking locally and around the U.S. with the family, and
providing engineering support on odd projects to friends and family.
